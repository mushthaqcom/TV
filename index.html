How to watch on your TV or phone

Install an M3U player on your device and add the playlist link below to watch live TV.

  1. Install a player (TiviMate, IPTV Smarters, VLC, etc.).
  2. Choose Add Playlist or Add M3U in the app.
  3. Paste the playlist URL and save.
  4. Refresh channels and start watching.
